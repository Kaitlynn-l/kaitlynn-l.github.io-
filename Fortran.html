Fortran

Fortran is a programming language created by IBM. The name is an abreviation of "Formula translation."Their goal was to create a language that was easier to understand than the assembly language, which they formerly used in their computers. It is known for being one of the first languages to include a built-in compiler. It is commonly used for solving complex mathematical equasions, and is currantly used to benchmark modern day super computers.
