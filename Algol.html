Algol

Algol was origenally published in 1958. A team of American and European computer scientists created it at Swiss Federal Institute of Technology. Its purpose of creation was to aleviate all "problems" with Fortran. A little known fact is that Algol is the reason we have the languages we do today. It inspired the creation of Pascal and Java. These two languages then inspired other languages. Java inspired Javascript. After C, Objective C, C++, and C# were created.
